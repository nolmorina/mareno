## Prompt 1 — Interior Lounge Editorial Shot

```markdown
Create a premium fashion editorial image for a modern summer clothing brand using the provided clothing images as the exact garment references.

Use the uploaded clothing items and model exactly as shown. Do not redesign, reinterpret, or alter the garments. Preserve the real colors, fit, proportions, fabric appearance, and product identity.

Scene:
Place the model inside a refined, sunlit interior with a calm luxury atmosphere.

Background and environment:
- boutique hotel lounge or high-end Mediterranean villa interior
- warm beige plaster walls
- travertine or light oak flooring
- soft linen curtains
- minimal cream or sand-toned furniture
- subtle sunlight entering through large windows
- elegant but uncluttered interior styling

Model direction:
- relaxed standing posture
- confident but effortless expression
- natural luxury mood
- clothing remains the hero of the image

Composition:
- full body or three-quarter editorial framing
- soft natural light
- realistic shadows
- premium fashion campaign quality
- background softly visible but not distracting

Mood:
elevated, calm, modern, warm, resort-inspired, understated luxury

Negative guidance:
no busy decor, no dark interior, no logos, no text, no clutter, no distracting props
```
